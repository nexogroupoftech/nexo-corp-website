About Nexo.corp

Nexo.corp is a small, independent studio focused on building clean, practical and intelligent tools powered by AI. We care about clarity, usefulness and design more than buzzwords.

Our first public project is XO AI — a calm, professional assistant designed to help with study, problem solving, planning and daily life. Over time, we aim to grow a full ecosystem of tools that make knowledge and productivity easier for everyone.

Founder

Nexo.corp was founded by Dev, a student and self-taught builder who enjoys combining technology, design and psychology. The goal is simple: create products that feel respectful, focused and genuinely helpful.

What we value

  • Clarity — No fake hype. Just clear communication.
  • Respect — Tools that support people, not replace them.
  • Privacy — Minimal data collection, no selling of data.
  • Learning — We improve the products based on feedback.

This website and the tools hosted here are still early-stage experiments. Features may change frequently as we test, learn and improve.
